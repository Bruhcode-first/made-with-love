# For Menna — Written in Stars

A cinematic particle heart with her name in the sky, a letter, and your song.

## Full song (not Spotify preview)

Spotify’s embed only plays a **30-second preview** and won’t autoplay the full track on a website. To use **your** Spotify song in full:

1. Save the track as **`song.mp3`** (same song as your link in `script.js`).
2. Put `song.mp3` in this folder (next to `index.html`).
3. Keep your `SPOTIFY_URL` in `script.js` — it fills in album art and the “open in Spotify” button.

The song **auto-starts** when her name appears (~2.6 seconds after she taps **open the sky**).

**Volume:** use the slider on the now-playing card (top-right), or set the default in `script.js` with `SONG_VOLUME` (0–1). The slider choice is remembered in the browser.

## Preview

Open `index.html` in Chrome or Edge (sound works best after clicking **open the sky**).
